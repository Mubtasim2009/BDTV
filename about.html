About BDTV VOD

BDTV VOD is a browser-based interface for browsing movies, TV shows, and BDIX IPTV streams in a clean, dark layout. It combines The Movie Database (TMDB) metadata with third‑party streaming providers and BDIX playlists.

Features

  • Trending, top-rated, and now‑playing movie & TV sections.
  • Integrated search across movies and series.
  • Per‑title player pages for movies and TV series.
  • Live TV player with BDIX IPTV playlists and HLS support.

Data & Streaming

Metadata (titles, posters, overviews, ratings) is provided by TMDB tmdb.org, while actual streams are hosted by third‑party providers and BDIX playlists. BDTV VOD itself does not host or store any video content.

Disclaimer

Streams are provided by external hosts and BDIX playlists. Availability and quality can change at any time. If a channel or VOD title does not play correctly, it is usually an issue with the external source rather than BDTV VOD.
