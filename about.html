What is BDTV VOD?

BDTV VOD is a front‑end that lets you explore film and series information and open dedicated players for each title, while keeping the home screen focused on discovery.

You get trending, now‑playing and top‑rated rows, plus full details on separate movie and TV pages with artwork and descriptions.

Dark UI Movie & TV focus Multi‑page layout

Features

  • Trending movies & TV with both posters and backdrops.
  • Now Playing and Top Rated sections for quick discovery.
  • Dedicated movie players with title, year, rating and genres.
  • TV players with season & episode selectors styled to match the app.
  • Separate search page with filters for Movies, TV, or both.

How to use BDTV VOD

Start on the Home page to see trending rows and quick picks. Use Search when you know exactly what you’re looking for. Clicking any card opens a new player tab, so you can watch without losing your place on the home feed.

For TV shows, pick a season and episode in the selector bar above the player – the design matches the rest of BDTV VOD so it feels like one connected experience.
